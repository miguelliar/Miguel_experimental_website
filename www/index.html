A brief introduction

Hi! If you are reading this page maybe is because you found it by chance, or you are just taking a brief look about what in earth I am developing. Well, fortunately for you, in this webpage you can take a brief look about myself, the webpage developed and some information.

Then, let's start!

Relationship with nature

Since I was a child I've always loved nature. I fortunately live in Asturias where having grass fields, forests, wildlife and beautiful landscapes around you is as easy as looking around. I also have been living in a small and quiet village, 15 minutes away from Oviedo, that helped me to connect with my environment and love where I live.

In my free time admiring the beauty of nature, no matter if it's cloudy, rainy and grey or sunny and full of life is one of my most favourite ways of disconnect from the stress and leave my mind free.

Walking around

Loving nature is a perfect complement with loving walking. Going into mountains, routes, discovering new places, feeling the wind while walking and enjoying brief pauses admiring the landscape are some of many thrills of walking.

For example, one of my favourite experiences was the "Camino de Santiago". I've done it two different times by two different paths, the coast and the interior path. It was an awesome experience, full of joy, meeting new amazing people and having amazing experiences.

Drawing and painting

Writing about my hobbies and the things I love and not mentioning drawing and painting would feel like going to the beach and not eating ice cream. Painting and specially drawing has always been a way of expressing my feelings and way of living things. I've been drawing since I could grab a pen and despite having seasons of being more productive or creative, it may be the only hobby that I actually maintain actively for a that long period of time.

I went to public painting activities in the public library from the age of 4 to 7 and after that I went to private classes. Since then until I was 18 years old I attended to Oviedo to an artist that taught me almost everything I know today. She taught me to paint in oil, improve my drawing and depth perception and to be constant.

Nowadays I don't have all time I would like to draw and paint, but I try to keep on with it and spend some time every few weeks at least.

Traveling

Who doesn't like to travel? Well, in my case I am very fortunate as I started traveling with my family very soon. We usually made day trips or take small vacations in different parts of Spain. I have very good memories about those trips. Even more, at the age of 10, my mother-part family started making cruise vacations and, fortunately, I was able to go with them.

We went through a great part of the Mediterranean sea, we went through the Norwegian fjord, the Baltic sea and the Atlantic sea. Until 2017 we visited almost every place in a cruise ship. However, despite the privilege that it was, you could only visit certain parts of the city. You could not appreciate the totality of the places, getting with a small picture of some gorgeous cities and places.

In 2017 I made my last travel with my family. We went to Thailand and my mother organized every aspect of the vacations. It was an unforgettable experience. We stayed for two weeks and visited Bangkok, the island of Phuket and Chiang Mai.

Last time I went out of Spain was in 2018, where I went to visit a friend in Abu Dhabi in Arabia Saudi, where I stayed for a week and a half during Christmas. It was the first time I traveled abroad alone, and I loved it.

Now, due to the pandemic and lack of time I haven't been able to travel as much as I would like. However, I am looking forward to visiting and staying more time in new places and discover its culture, gastronomy and history (and making a travel journal).

Family

Despite being an only child, my family is huge on both sides of the tree. On the one hand, we have my mother family from Asturias. They are 5 direct brothers and many other members (which I don't know or remember well). I live close to all them, and they work together in a family business.

On the other hand, we have my father family from Andalucía. They are distributed over all Andalucía, but they usually stay near Seville where we met every holiday. They are 5 brothers too and everyone has a child around my age, so I spend a lot of time with them.
